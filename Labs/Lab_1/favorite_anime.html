Title: Bakemonogatari

Genre: Mystery, Supernatural, Romance

Theme: Vampire

Studio: Shaft

Rating: Mature 17+

A Brief Synopsis: Koyomi Araragi, a high school student, tries to lead a normal life, but the supernatural seems to be specifically attracted to him and those around him in his home town. Follow alongside him as he supports foes and friends alike in their supernatural afflictions.

Title: Fate/Zero

Genre: Action, Fantasy

Theme: Urban Fantasy

Studio: ufotable

Rating: Mature 17+

A Brief Synopsis: A prequel to the anime adaptation of the hit visual novel Fate/stay night. The story centers around an event called the Holy Grail War, the 4th one in particular, where seven "masters" contracted with servants fight one another in a battle royale style fight to claim the Holy Grail: an artifact with the capability to grant any one wish to the master who comes out victorious. We follow Emiya Kiritsugu, one of the seven masters part of this war, as he fights his way through to the top to claim the almighty grand prize.

Title: Attack on Titan

Genre: Action, Drama, Suspense

Themes: Gore, Military, Survival

Studio: Wit Studio

Rating: Mature 17+

A Brief Synopsis: Life behind monolithic walls is the only way for humanity to get by after the sudden appearance of the equally colossal beasts called titans. Eren Yeager, one such resident of the walled city, has joined the Survey Corps to aid the effort to reclaim the land that was lost from the titanic takeover. Whilst in this military unit, many secrets are uncovered about the truth and origin of these beasts.

Title: Jojo's Bizarre Adventure

Genre: Action, Adventure, Supernatural

Themes: Vampire, Historical

Studio: David Production

Rating: Mature 17+

A Brief Synopsis: This story and the ones proceeding it follow the Joestar family across multiple generations, each member possessing unique powers and abilities. Through countless encounters with supernatural beings, "stand" users, and the like, there's plenty interesting going on in this often times vulgar adventure.

Title: Frieren: Beyond Journey's End

Genre: Adventure, Fantasy, Drama

Themes: Magic, Medieval

Studio: Madhouse

Rating: PG-13

A Brief Synopsis: We follow one of the four legendary party members who slayed the Demon King years ago, Frieren, as she has a newfound goal in understanding humans better by making connections with them after taking for granted the time she spent with her fellow party members, who have either passed on or are very close to death's door.
